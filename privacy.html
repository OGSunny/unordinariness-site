Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 9, 2025

Your privacy is important. This bot verifies Roblox accounts for Discord role assignment.

What We Collect

  • Discord User ID (for verification).
  • Roblox ID and Username (from OAuth login, stored in secure MongoDB).
  • Verification timestamp.

We do not collect emails, IPs, or personal details beyond what's needed for verification.

How We Use Data

Data is used only to link accounts and assign roles based on Roblox groups. No sharing with third parties.

Storage & Security

Data is stored in MongoDB Atlas (encrypted, compliant with privacy laws). You can unverify to delete it via /unverify.

Your Rights

Request deletion anytime. Complies with applicable laws (e.g., GDPR). Contact: jemalagegidze@gmail.com
