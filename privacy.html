🔒Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 9, 2025
Effective Date: September 9, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how RoxTether collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot verification services.

📊 Information We Collect

We collect only essential information needed to provide verification services between Discord and Roblox:

Data Type Purpose Source Storage Duration
Discord User ID Link Discord account to verification Discord API Until unverified
Roblox User ID Verify group membership and ranks Roblox OAuth Until unverified
Roblox Username Display verification information Roblox OAuth Until unverified
Verification Timestamp Track verification status System Generated Until unverified
Server Configurations Role bindings and settings Discord Commands Until bot removal

🛡️ What We DON'T Collect

  • Email addresses or personal contact information
  • IP addresses (beyond standard server logs)
  • Personal messages or Discord chat history
  • Roblox passwords or authentication credentials
  • Financial information or payment data
  • Device information or browser data

🔄 How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for providing verification services:

Account Verification

Securely linking your Discord and Roblox accounts through official OAuth authentication.

Role Management

Automatically assigning Discord roles based on your current Roblox group memberships and ranks.

Service Operation

Maintaining bot functionality, resolving technical issues, and preventing service abuse.

💾 Data Storage and Security

🔐 Security Implementation

  • Encryption: All data stored with AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • OAuth Security: Official Roblox OAuth 2.0 - zero password storage
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on secure MongoDB Atlas with enterprise-grade protection
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and automated threat detection

Data Location: Your information is stored on MongoDB Atlas servers in secure data centers with SOC 2 Type II compliance and industry-leading security standards.

🤝 Data Sharing Policy

We DO NOT sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

Legal Disclosure Exception

We may disclose information only if legally required by court order or law enforcement, with advance notice to users when legally permissible.

⚖️ Your Privacy Rights

  • Instant Deletion: Use /unverify to immediately delete all verification data
  • Data Access: Use /whois @user to view stored verification information
  • Data Portability: Contact us to request a complete copy of your stored data
  • Correction Rights: Re-verify using /reverify to update your information
  • Service Withdrawal: Remove the bot from your server to delete all associated data

🗑️ Simple Data Deletion

Complete data removal is instant and user-controlled:

  • Run /unverify command in Discord
  • All verification data is permanently deleted within seconds
  • Discord roles may be removed based on server settings
  • No residual data remains in our systems

📱 Third-Party Integrations

RoxTether integrates with the following services to provide verification functionality:

  • Discord API: For bot operations, role management, and user interactions
  • Roblox API/OAuth: For secure account verification and group data retrieval
  • MongoDB Atlas: For secure data storage and management

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Discord's and Roblox's privacy statements for comprehensive understanding.

👶 Children's Privacy Protection

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with COPPA requirements. Users under 13 should have parental guidance when using our service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental consent.

🔄 Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect service improvements or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated through our official channels with reasonable advance notice. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance of revised terms.
