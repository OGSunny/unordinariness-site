Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Vertex Security ("we," "us," or "our") handles the information we collect and process when you use our Discord bot and related services (collectively, the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide and improve the Vertex Security bot's functionality. This includes:

  • Discord User IDs: We store user IDs to track moderation actions (e.g., warnings, mutes, bans), risk scores, and ban appeals. This allows us to apply and manage punishments and provide a ban appeal system.
  • Discord Server IDs: We store server IDs to maintain server-specific settings and configurations, such as security levels, logging channels, and custom filters.
  • Moderation Logs: We log moderation actions (who was punished, by whom, and for what reason) to provide a transparent and accountable record for server owners and administrators. These logs are not shared publicly.
  • Non-Personalized Aggregated Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated data about command usage and feature adoption to help us understand how the bot is used and to guide future development. This data cannot be used to identify any individual user.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the Vertex Security bot, including moderation, raid protection, and content filtering.
  • To enforce server-specific rules and security settings configured by server administrators.
  • To manage the ban appeal process, allowing users to communicate with the moderation team.
  • To analyze and improve the performance and features of the Service.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your data with any third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Discord, Inc.: Our bot operates on the Discord platform and relies on Discord's API to function. Your data is subject to Discord's privacy policy.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain collected data for as long as it is necessary to provide the Service. If you remove the bot from a server, all associated server-specific data will be deleted within 30 days. We take reasonable measures to protect the information we hold, but no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your data that we hold. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our official Discord support server.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
