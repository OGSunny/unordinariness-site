3. Verification Process and Role Management

3.1 Account Verification

Users verify their identity through Roblox's official OAuth system. This process securely links your Discord account to your Roblox account without sharing sensitive credentials. Verification data is stored securely and used solely for role assignment purposes.

3.2 Role Assignment

  • Roblox group membership status
