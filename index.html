Setup in Minutes

Get your community verified in just four simple steps

1

Invite RoxTether

Add the bot to your server with the required permissions. Our intelligent permission system requests only what's needed for seamless operation.

2

Configure Group Bindings

Link your Roblox groups with Discord roles using /bind [group_id] [min_rank] [discord_role] - supports complex hierarchies and multiple groups.

3

Members Verify

Users run /verify and complete secure OAuth authentication. No passwords shared, no security risks.

4

Enjoy Automation

Roles are assigned instantly with continuous synchronization. Your community management is now completely automated and secure.
