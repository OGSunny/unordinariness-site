Powerful Features

Vertex Security is built with a suite of next-gen tools to ensure your community is protected 24/7.

AI Anti-Raid System

Our AI-powered system actively monitors your server for suspicious join activity. If it detects a raid—defined as 5 or more joins within a short 30-second window—it instantly activates **lockdown mode**. New members are automatically greeted with a CAPTCHA verification process, while existing members can continue to chat freely, ensuring minimal disruption to your community. This system is crucial for stopping malicious bot raids and coordinated human attacks before they can cause widespread damage.

Multilingual Content Filter

Hate speech and toxicity know no language barriers. Our advanced content filter uses a combination of machine learning and over 1,000 regex patterns to detect and remove harmful content, including slurs and spam, in more than 50 languages. The bot automatically deletes violating messages and can be configured to escalate punishments from a simple warning to a kick or a ban based on a user's history.

Real-time Risk Scoring

Vertex assigns a **risk score** to every user in your server, based on factors like account age, activity patterns, and past moderation actions. This proactive system helps identify potential troublemakers before they can cause problems. If a user's risk score crosses a configurable threshold (e.g., 50), the bot can be set to automatically take action, such as quarantining them or applying a pre-set punishment.

Streamlined Ban Appeals

Our system makes ban appeals easy and transparent. Instead of having to join a separate server, a banned user can simply DM the bot with the `!appeal` command. This opens a modal form where they can explain their situation. The appeal is then automatically forwarded to a designated moderation channel in your server, allowing your team to review and handle it efficiently.

Owner Private Logs

Full transparency is key to effective moderation. Vertex Security maintains a separate, hidden log channel visible only to server owners and other designated trusted staff. This channel logs all critical moderation actions, including bans, unbans, and configurations, ensuring you have a complete, tamper-proof record of what happens in your server, even if other logs are deleted.
